OBITUARY OF NOMPHELO BEAUTY TALENI

Nomphelo Beauty Taleni was born to Theophilus Nkosentsha Taleni and Rhoda Tenjiwe Taleni (n√©e Luwaca) in 1948 at Godini in Ngqeleni. She was the 11th child of 13 siblings.


Nomphelo's academic journey began at Godini JSS, where she completed her primary education. She then attended Buntingville SSS in Ngqeleni, before pursuing a Teacher's Diploma at Shawbury Teachers College, graduating in 1968. Her dedication to education led her to teach at various schools, including Ntlaza JSS, Godini JSS, and Mbange JSS in the Libode District.


In 1989, Nomphelo founded Mbekweni Senior Primary School, where she served as principal until her retirement in 2012. Her tireless efforts and commitment to education left an indelible mark on the lives of countless learners and Maqhinebeni Location.


Nomphelo is survived by her two children, Luthando (Boy) and Msawenkosi; five siblings; seven grandchildren; and one great-grandchild. Her eldest child, Vuyokazi, predeceased her.


The family and community mourn the loss of this extraordinary woman, whose legacy will continue to inspire and educate future generations. Rest in peace, Ndlangisa, Thole, Mthombeni.
